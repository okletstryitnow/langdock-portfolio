**User Query:** "How many vacation days am I entitled to?"
**Expected Output:**

- Mention source (`urlaubsregelung.md`).

**User Query:** "I want to work from Italy for 2 weeks this summer. Is that possible?"
**Expected Output:**
- Confirm: **Yes**, up to 4 weeks/year in EU countries allowed.
- Condition: Agreement with team lead.
- Source: `remote-work-policy.md`.
